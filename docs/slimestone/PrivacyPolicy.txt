Privacy Policy

This application, “Slime and Stone”, is developed by Araya.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and stored when you use our game.

Information We Collect

The game collects gameplay-related data to support account-based progress saving.
This may include:
- Game progress
- Player achievements
- In-game settings or unlocks

This data is associated with the user’s account to allow progress recovery.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or precise location.

How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used solely to:
- Save and restore game progress
- Allow users to recover their previous gameplay data after reinstalling the game
- Improve game stability and user experience

When a user reinstalls the game and signs in with the same account, previously saved gameplay data will be restored.

Data Storage and Retention

Gameplay data is securely stored on our servers or trusted third-party services.
Data is retained as long as the account remains active or until the user requests deletion.

If the game is uninstalled, the gameplay data may still be retained to support future restoration when using the same account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.
Data may only be processed by services necessary for cloud saving or account authentication.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
Data transmission is protected using standard security practices.

User Rights

Users may request:
- Access to their stored data
- Deletion of their account data

Requests can be made via the contact information below.

Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for general audiences.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
